📊 About the Algorithm

NeuroCluster Streamer (NCS) is a high-performance adaptive clustering algorithm that combines neural network-inspired memory mechanisms with clustering-based spatial reasoning for real-time pattern discovery in financial data streams.

Unlike traditional clustering methods that require predefined cluster counts and batch processing, NCS automatically discovers market regime patterns while adapting to changing conditions through a confidence-based decay mechanism.

🎯 Key Features

  • Vectorized NumPy operations for maximum performance
  • Dynamic threshold adaptation based on data characteristics
  • Multi-layer outlier detection (geometric, statistical, temporal)
  • Intelligent cluster health monitoring and management
  • Concept drift detection and adaptation
  • Memory-efficient bounded collections architecture

💹 Financial Applications

Algorithmic Trading: Market regime-based strategy switching and real-time risk management integration.

Quantitative Research: Live market microstructure analysis and strategy development enhancement.

Risk Management: Continuous monitoring of factor regimes for dynamic portfolio optimization.

🔬 Validation & Testing

The algorithm has been validated on live financial market data from major technology stocks:

  • AAPL (Apple Inc.)
  • GOOGL (Alphabet Inc.)
  • MSFT (Microsoft Corporation)
  • TSLA (Tesla Inc.)

Processing at 100-millisecond intervals with effective real-time adaptation to varying market conditions.
